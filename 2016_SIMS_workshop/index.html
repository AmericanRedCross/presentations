Principles and "Standardization"

Good maps come in all shapes and colours

Priorities

Timely

Pretty

Useful

  • Who will use this?
  • For what purpose?
  • In what format?
  • What do users need to orient themselves?
  • Is it simple enough?

Rules

  • Logos: IFRC, HNS, PNS
  • Text blob: borders disclaimer, data sources, production date, SIMS
  • Meaningful scale and units
  • Legend if necessary
  • Use bright colours sparingly. Make less important things lighter
  • Keep file size small enough to share
