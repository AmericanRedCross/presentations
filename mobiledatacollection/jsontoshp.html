GeoJson to Shapefile

Tips for converting geojson to shapefile

  1. Download and install a text editor such as Notepad++
  2. Open your GeoJson in Notepad++
  3. Copy the text that appears
  4. Go to ogr2ogr web client
  5. On the right side you’ll see an option for “Convert from GeoJson”
  6. Paste the text in the “GEOJSON” text space
  7. Give your file a name in the “Output Name” text space
  8. Click “Convert to Shapefile”
  9. Your file will download to your Downloads folder
