Shelter Programming

What is Shelter?

IFRC Definition

A shelter is a secure habitable covered living space providing privacy and dignity for those within it

Core considerations

  • Protection from climate
  • Privacy and dignity
  • Personal safety and security

Types of shelter

  • Emergency
  • Temporary / Transitional
  • Permanent

Technical areas of focus (pt 1)

  • Proper drainage system around the shelter
  • Adequate foundation depth
  • Spanning
  • Walls / roof joinery
  • Bracing techniques

Technical areas of focus (pt 2)

  • Disaster-specific preparedness measures
  • Adequate spacing between buildings
  • Land tenure
