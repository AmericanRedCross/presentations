Introductions and informed consent

Interview the head of household if possible, an adult if not

Household head


Responsible for care and organization of household. Male or female

Do not interview children! Move on and return later.

Greet and ask for permission / consent to interview

Answer any questions they have

General guidelines

Answer all questions

Conduct surveys in English. Switch to Tagalog version to clarify confusing questions.

Use a neutral voice and NEVER provide personal impressions

Only rephrase if the question confuses the respondent

Don't hurry the interview

Don't change the wording of questions

Sometimes "I don't know" = confusion. Check!

Don't take answers not from the respondent

Only you complete the questionnaire, no one else

For "don't know" answers to number questions write 99

Practice survey!
