What's next?

We've accomplished a lot in the past few months but the next year is going to be even bigger for us.

Launching Several New Projects

We are launching several initatives.

GeoDB

The GeoDB project is meant to help us know where we are and where we are doing activities, to help us tie ECOS and location together. The GeoDB contains over 10 million locations with the ability to add new locations as they come up. This is the first phase of our technology scale-up.

Web Visualization

We are starting an initative to allow you to visualize GIS data more easily. The 2nd Phase of our technology scale-up is a website that will perform visualizations of ECOS data to allow you to perform a very quick spatial analysis. We are also prepping to give you a website to drag and drop simple GIS data to very quickly visualize and produce GIS information.

Data Governance

Sometimes things just don't look quite right. We get asked make a lot of maps from data collected in the field from a lot of different national societies and NHQ offices. Standardizing data is really important to us because it allows us to perform analysis faster. Over the next year we are going to be working to create data standards and provide templates to ISD so we can do our jobs better and faster.
