what's next?

We've accomplished a lot in the past few months but the next year is going to be even bigger for us.

lauching new projects

We are launching several initatives.

geodb

The GeoDB project is meant to help us know where we are and where we are doing activities, to help us tie ECOS and location together. The GeoDB contains over 10 million locations with the ability to add new locations as they come up. This is the first phase of our technology scale-up.

web viz

Web Viz We are starting an initative to allow you to visualize GIS data more easily. The 2nd Phase of our technology scale-up is a website that will perform visualizations of ECOS data to allow you to perform a very quick spatial analysis. We are also prepping to give you a website to drag and drop simple GIS data to very quickly visualize and produce GIS information.

info management

Sometimes things just don't look right. We get asked make a lot of maps from data collected in the field from a lot of different national societies and NHQ offices. Standardizing data is really important to us because it allows us to perform analysis faster. Over the next year we are going to be working to create data standards and provide templates to ISD so we can do our jobs better and faster.

in the room

In the room GIS often gets brought into the situation after a program or project is done. We want to change that. We want to be in the room when decisions are made to help improve, track, and facilitate projects throughout the entire projects.

use cases

Use Cases We are developing Use Cases to help show how GIS can enhance and inform projects in different ways and at different times.

sops

SOPs We are also developing Standard Operating Procedures around the types of products we can create. As you've seen we can make everything from print maps to guides and even quick web maps and applications. We are really scalling up and want all of you to be partners.
Thanks a bunch and come talk to us if you have ideas or want our feedback on anything mapping related.
